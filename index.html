John Hur

Born in Germany June 28, 1990, John "the Machine" Hur began his crazy existence.

John did not know what he wanted to do with his life, but he knew two things: He wanted to help and create.

John helped by dabbling in social work, working in nursing homes and residential treatment centers for traumatized youths. John helped both the old and the young.

John loved creating. John wrote sophomorically. John sang horribly. John danced befuddlingly. And recently, John coded.

John will continue to help and create, but he hopes to do so with the magic of coding!

HTML! CSS! JAVASCRIPT! NODE! EXPRESS! SQL! All languages at his fingertips! Yet, he hungers for more.

Dibs

People are not great. Unfortunately, we usually have to share a whole apartment building with other people. That probably means we use the same laundry room. Wouldn't it be nice if you can reserve a laundry machine and avoid all your quirky neighbors. Now you can! With Dibs, you can make reservations for washing/drying machines. Gone are the days of seeing your floor in a pile next to the trash can. No more missing of a singular sock. Maybe the best way to co-exist is to avoid people altogether. Thank you, Dibs!
